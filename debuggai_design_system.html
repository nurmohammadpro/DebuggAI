What is Zero-Knowledge Mode? ▾
Zero-Knowledge Mode ensures your code and error logs are never stored on our servers. All analysis happens in-memory and is immediately discarded after the response is delivered. Available on Pro and Enterprise plans.
💳
How do credits work? ▾
Each debug analysis costs 1 credit. Reverse builder costs 25 credits. Multi-target export costs 35 credits. Credits reset monthly with your subscription. Free tier includes 30 credits per month.
⚡
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? ▾
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your current billing period. You keep your credits until they expire.
